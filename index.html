Visual Utility Technological Aid

(V.U.T.A.)

Visual Utility Technological Aid is an Electronic Travel Aid or (ETA) designed to help the visually impaired avoid obstacles with the help of digital camera that foresees the object and collision in front of the impaired person serving as the temporary eyes for the blind.
